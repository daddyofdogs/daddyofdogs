Our Commitment to Accessibility

Effective Date: August 13, 2025
Last Updated: August 13, 2025

DADDYOFDOGS is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities in accordance with the German Barrierefreiheitsstärkungsgesetz (BFSG), implementing EU Directive 2016/2102 on the accessibility of websites and mobile applications.

Technical Standards and Compliance

This website strives to conform to the EN 301 549 standard and Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA criteria. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with disabilities.

Current Compliance Status

Partially Compliant - Some parts of the content do not fully conform to the accessibility standard due to the issues outlined below.

Assessment Methodology

This accessibility statement was prepared based on:

  • Assessment Type: Internal evaluation with external accessibility audit
  • Assessment Date: August 2025
  • Methodology: Automated testing tools, manual testing, and assistive technology evaluation
  • Technical Standards: EN 301 549 v3.2.1, WCAG 2.1 Level AA

Known Accessibility Issues

We are working to address the following accessibility barriers:

Content Limitations

  • Some older PDF documents may not be fully screen reader compatible
  • Certain embedded third-party content may have accessibility limitations
  • Some video content may lack comprehensive audio descriptions

Planned Improvements

  • PDF accessibility remediation: Target completion Q4 2025
  • Video content enhancement: Ongoing process
  • Third-party content accessibility review: Q1 2026

Enforcement and Dispute Resolution

Internal Complaints Process

If you encounter accessibility barriers or wish to request information in an alternative format, please contact us using the methods above. We are committed to providing accessible alternatives and addressing accessibility concerns promptly.

External Arbitration Service

If you believe we have not responded adequately to your accessibility concerns, you may contact the German arbitration service:

Schlichtungsstelle nach dem Behindertengleichstellungsgesetz (BGG)

  • Address: Mauerstraße 53, 10117 Berlin, Germany
  • Phone: +49 (0)30 18 527-2805
  • Email: info@schlichtungsstelle-bgg.de
  • Website: www.schlichtungsstelle-bgg.de

Important: This arbitration service is free of charge and does not require legal representation. The service helps resolve disputes between individuals with disabilities and public or private service providers regarding accessibility barriers.

Working Together for an Inclusive Future

Unsere Verpflichtung zur Barrierefreiheit

Wirksamkeitsdatum: 13. August 2025

Diese Website strebt die Einhaltung des EN 301 549-Standards und der Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA-Kriterien an. Diese Richtlinien erklären, wie Webinhalte für Menschen mit Behinderungen zugänglicher gemacht werden können.

Aktueller Konformitätsstatus

Teilweise konform - Einige Teile des Inhalts entsprechen nicht vollständig dem Barrierefreiheitsstandard aufgrund der unten aufgeführten Probleme.
